  The RestViz NPM package is a plug-and-play middleware for Express (v4 & v5) that auto-generates interactive API documentation. No need for Swagger or Postman — just drop it in your app, and you're good to go. It’s fast, framework-agnostic, and focused on developer ergonomics.
